Smart Irrigation

🌱

Crop Monitoring

Research Problem

Traditional irrigation methods lead to water wastage and inconsistent crop yields. This research addresses the need for automated, data-driven irrigation systems that can adapt to soil conditions and weather patterns.

Methodology

Implemented IoT sensors for soil moisture, temperature, and humidity monitoring. Used machine learning algorithms to predict optimal irrigation schedules based on historical data and real-time conditions.

Results

Achieved 30% water savings and 25% improvement in crop yield compared to traditional methods. The system demonstrated 95% accuracy in predicting irrigation needs across different crop types.
